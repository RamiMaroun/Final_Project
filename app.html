Registration

The registration can be done at the touch of a button, by logging in with your email or Facebook account. If you choose to sign up with Facebook account, it will only retrieve your first name.

Registration as advisor

If you are professional and would like to be a volunteer, you can send us a registration form. After you have completed your registration as advisor, we will let you know soon as possible once we have reviewed it.

NB! As you register yourself as advisor, you need to upload your documents.

Homepage

Once you sing in, it will open the home page where you can see many shared experiences of VAW. You can click plus button to share your experience or others experiences of VAW. In addition you can click on anonymous mode button to hide your real identity if you would like.

Menu

By clicking on the menu, you will get more alternatives.

psychological counselling

You can choose either to chat or call a specialist psychological. The call will be secret and secure from the application directly, so you do not need to add your telephone number.

Legal advice

Once you click on legal advice, You can choose also either to chat or call one of them.
