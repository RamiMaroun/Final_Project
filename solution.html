combating violence against women

Our idea is based on the principle of combating violence against women, by creating a website and mobile application, through which it publishes personal experiences of women's victims of violence, in which the user chooses to publish anonymously or not, with a hashtag to support the case, and to provide advice to other people on how to act in such cases. The user can receive replays from other users as well.

Through the system, psychologists, legal, or health professionals, in general, can provide guidance and support through their own control panel through which they can answer users' inquiries. Registration of specialists in the system is not done randomly, as those interested (volunteers) must send to the site management certificates or documents that prove their competence.

Our app combating VAW contributes to estimating the cases and causes of violence against women around the world. Many violence cases against women are unreported because of many reasons. Shame, fear and lack of knowledge about rights make women reluctant to report incidents to the police. The app can help us to gather information about VAW in these countries where women exposed to various violence without reporting.
